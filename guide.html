How To Play STAH Game

  • Initially, there will be n stones on the table
  • There will be a certain set of m numbers
  • Among the m numbers, there is always number 1
  • There will be two players, you and Cipio, who move alternately
  • The legal moves consist in removing stones by one of m numbers
  • The move is legal if its number is not greater than the number of stones left
  • The winner is the one who takes the last stone

Example:
Let n be 10 and there are 3 numbers: 1,3,8. It means that both players only allowed to remove the stones by 1, 3, or 8.
Consider you are the first one to take the stones.

No. Player Move Stones Left Note
1 You 8 2 In next turn, 3 and 8 will be illegal moves
2 Cipio 1 1 1 is the only legal move left
3 You 1 0 Take the last stone and yeah, you win the game!
