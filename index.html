Welcome to STAH Game

Cipio is a smart boy. One day, he wanted to create a fascinating game. And, voila, this is the game, STAH Game!.
Apparently, despite being smart, Cipio is a lazy boy. Sometimes, he is in the mood to play the game, sometimes he isn't.
Now, he is challenging you to play the game. Can you ALWAYS defeat him while he is in his mood to play the game? Let's have a try.

If it is your first time playing this game, you should read the rule of the game in the How To Play page.
Anyway, you can read the story of the game in the Story page or you can read the credit in the About page.
