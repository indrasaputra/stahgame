Story of STAH Game

One day, Cipio, a logic lover, joined an amazing competition called National Logic Competition (NLC), a competition for junior and senior high school student held by Schematics, an annual event of Department of Informatics, Faculty of Information Technology, Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember.
He managed to enter the semifinal round easily. In this round, he had to play a game with the committee. He played his best but still he couldn't defeat the committee. As you can guess, he failed to enter the final round.
Several months later, he read a book about game theory and he was shocked because the solution of the game he played in the semifinal round was in that book! So, he learnt and learnt. Then, he created a game based on the game he played before. He named it 'STAH Game' because whenever he felt useless, the word 'STAH' always come out from his mouth.

Note: Sometimes, Cipio is kind enough to let you win the game if you do your best. Good luck!
